# AUDIT_WP-2026-157 - ECC capability pack

## Audit Focus

Verify that the ticket adds useful ECC-inspired capabilities without broadening runtime risk or coupling.

## What Must Be True

- `deep-research` is a pure documentation skill with clear triggers and a structured output.
- `ap-schema.md` and `validate_observations.py` agree on the contract for persistent observations.
- `tests/evals/` covers the key orchestration contracts with isolated fixtures.
- The `eval` marker is registered and the eval suite is explicit.
- The research output path is gitignored and does not pollute the working tree.

## Failure Modes

1. The research skill is discoverable but not actually useful or structured.
2. The observation validator is too weak to catch malformed data.
3. The eval harness depends on live subprocesses or the production bus.
4. The standard suite becomes coupled to the eval harness.
5. The research output path lands in git and creates accidental churn.

## Review Checklist

- Confirm the skill is registered in the catalog and writes to the documented research path.
- Confirm the validator rejects malformed observations with a non-zero exit code and checks the required fields.
- Confirm the eval tests are isolated and deterministic, and each includes a negative case.
- Confirm the safe suite and eval suite remain separable.
- Confirm `.gitignore` excludes `.agent/runtime/research/`.
